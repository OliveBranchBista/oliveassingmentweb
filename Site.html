MAY 2022

My Experience Of Learning:

I had always been very interested in technology.I enjoy learning new things
and developing my academic skills.This was the first time I was introduced
with 'coding'. In the beginning it was little confusing and spending more time
in front of laptop screen,doing coding was eye tiring and hectic. But slowly
I keep on practicing and I started getting used to it and now it has become one
of my favourite thing to do.
I started realizing that there are so many important things that one should learn
while developing web.Starting with HTML I had already basic knowledge about
it since my secondary level education.So, it became quite easier for me but here
comes CSS which was completely new to me.First few days is was difficult for
me to understand the concepts of CSS.But the slides provided by the university
was really helpful to clear my doubts also the lecturer helped me every time I am
in confusion. Shadow Effect, 2D Transform, 3D Transform, Transitions, Animations
are the topics I found mostly unique and felt like doing it time and again.

SECTION 2

My Design Decisions:

I designed navigation bar containing five web pages like: home page, portfolio
page,contact page, blog page and site report. As I'm nature loving person,
I choosed natural theme for my portfolio site. I downloaded some copyright free
images from google and created a suitable background for each pages. Furthermore,
I choosed only those colours which matched the background.

In the home page I added my name in large font size with blur filter, and use of
hover effect plus proper use of transformation.

I also made a small section named 'My Gallery' where I designed my page using my
own clicked pictures. I also included animation and transition to the heading
'My Gallery". I Implemented shadow effects as well throughout the website.

Then, I designed footer adding quick access to the pages , my contact details with
my location. Overall, my website design is influenced by nature theme and
implementation of my HTML and CSS knowledge as much as possible.
